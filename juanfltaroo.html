Who is Juanflatroo?

Juanflatroo or his real name Flatron Halimi is a professional CS:GO player who comes from Kosova and lives in the capital city, Prishtina. Flatroo started playing the game and started dominating players with his best mate Sinnopsyy and then together reached the top players league called FPL in just 1 year of playing.
